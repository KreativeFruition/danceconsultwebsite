Meet Our Consultants

Industry experts with real-world experience ready to guide your creative journey.

Kevin Frey

Founder & Lead Consultant

Kevin Frey is the powerhouse behind 1:1 Dance Consult. With 15+ years in entertainment, he's danced for icons like Lady Gaga, Taylor Swift, Ariana Grande, and J Balvin—and directed high-impact projects across stage and screen. As founder, Kevin brings unmatched industry insight and sharp creative strategy to every session. He helps dancers, choreographers, and creatives break through plateaus, pivot with purpose, and elevate their careers. With his guidance, clients don't just dream bigger—they move smarter.

Montana Efaw

Choreographer + Movement Director

With over a decade of high‑pressure choreography and movement direction—from global tours and music videos to award shows—Montana empowers clients to fully own their stage presence. As Lady Gaga's longtime movement collaborator and key creative behind rising stars like Maya B, Allison Ponthier & SAINT JHN, she brings strategic artistic development and performance mastery to every project. Clients gain next‑level confidence, finely tuned movement identity, and stage‑ready storytelling through her elite coaching and director‑led vision.

Caroline Diamond

Multi-Hyphenate Artist

Caroline is a multi-hyphenate artist with credits including A Star Is Born, The L Word: Gen Q, and Lady Gaga's Super Bowl Halftime Show. With a background in choreography, movement coaching, styling, and certified makeup artistry, she helps clients align every part of their creative identity—from stage presence to camera-ready looks. She's also the creator and host of SOYEAH The Podcast, where creativity and real talk collide. Whether you're prepping for an audition, shoot, or building a brand, Caroline brings expert guidance and vision to help you show up fully expressed.
